Maria Paola Priola

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Cagliari, with a deep interest in the intersection between informatics, statistics, and econometrics. My current research focuses on the use of artificial intelligence and computational statistics, with a particular attention to applications in the healthcare domain.

Previously, I worked as an econometrician and data scientist at Prometeia (Bologna, Italy), where I specialized in natural language processing, machine learning, and financial forecasting for central banks and monetary policy institutions.

I earned a degree in Data Science during the second year of my PhD, and I hold advanced degrees in both Economics and Data Science. I have authored peer-reviewed publications in macroeconomics and sentiment analysis applications.
